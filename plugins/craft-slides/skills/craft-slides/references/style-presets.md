# Style presets

Starting points, not skins. Pick the one whose mood fits the subject, then adapt its palette
and type — or design a fresh direction in the same spirit. Read at Phase 2–3.

## Anti-slop rules (always)

- **Fonts:** never Inter, Roboto, Arial, or a system font as the *display* face. Choose a
  face with personality. For Thai, pair a Latin display with a Thai companion (see
  `thai-bilingual.md`).
- **Colour:** no `#6366f1` generic indigo, no purple-gradient-on-white. Commit to a
  dominant colour with one or two sharp accents, set in CSS variables.
- **Layout:** not everything centered, not identical card grids on every slide. Vary rhythm —
  a statement slide, a stat band, a quote, a comparison.
- **Atmosphere over flat fill:** layered gradients, a faint motif, grain, or a single graphic
  device beats a solid background. One well-staged page-load reveal beats scattered micro-
  animations.
- **Decoration with intent:** no gratuitous glassmorphism, no drop shadows without purpose,
  no stock illustrations. Abstract/CSS shapes and real content imagery only.

## Choosing by mood

| The subject should feel… | Reach for |
| --- | --- |
| Confident, modern, design-led | Signal, Brass Ledger |
| Calm, literary, premium | Washi, Botanical Dark |
| Technical, precise, engineered | Blueprint, Terminal |
| Warm, human, approachable | Riso, Washi |
| Bold, loud, poster-like | Broadsheet |

## The presets

Each is a launch pad — fonts (Google/Fontshare), a palette, and one signature device.

### Signal — confident editorial
Display **Fraunces** · body **Hanken Grotesk** · mono **Space Mono**. Off-white `#f4f1ea`,
ink `#16130f`, one hot accent (oxblood `#a23b2c` or cobalt `#2f53d8`). Signature: a thick
accent rule under section numbers; large italic display.

### Brass Ledger — warm dark premium
Display **Cormorant Garamond** (italic) · body **DM Sans** · mono **Space Mono**. Warm
near-black `#15120c`, ivory `#f1e7d2`, brass `#c79a45`. Signature: hairline gold rules, a
faint motif watermark, generous negative space.

### Washi — calm warm paper
Display **Newsreader** or **Fraunces** · body **Hanken Grotesk**. Washi cream `#f6f1e7`,
warm grey ink `#2b2622`, muted sage `#6f8f6a`. Signature: paper texture, soft serif, lots of
air. Light theme.

### Botanical Dark — elegant, atmospheric
Display **Cormorant** · body **IBM Plex Sans**. Near-black `#0f0f0f`, bone `#e8e4df`, warm
accents (terracotta `#d4a574`, dusty pink). Signature: blurred abstract gradient circles, a
thin vertical accent line. No illustrations.

### Blueprint — engineered, studious
Display **Space Grotesk** · body **IBM Plex Sans** · mono **IBM Plex Mono**. Graph-paper
ground (`#f3f4f0` with a faint grid), ink, one cobalt accent. Signature: hairline grid,
mono captions, dimension-line motifs.

### Terminal — developer, hacker
Mono **JetBrains Mono** throughout (pair **Bai Jamjuree** for Thai). GitHub-dark `#0d1117`,
terminal green `#39d353`. Signature: scan lines, a blinking cursor, syntax-coloured code.

### Riso — crafted, lo-fi warm
Display **Bricolage Grotesque** · body **Hanken Grotesk**. Beige `#efe7d6`, ink, one riso
accent (green `#3a7d44` or pink). Signature: slight mis-registration overlap, halftone
texture, hand-warm.

### Broadsheet — bold poster
Display **Anton** or **Archivo Black** (pair **Kanit** for Thai) · body **Work Sans**. Paper
white, ink, one fire accent `#e23b2c`. Signature: massive headline type, a few words per
slide, newspaper rules. Low density — statements, not paragraphs.

## Custom directions

If the subject has a sharper opportunity than any preset — a chess deck wanting an
ivory-and-ink board motif, a fintech deck wanting a ledger grid — design it. Hold the same
discipline: one distinctive display face, a committed palette, a recognizable layout system,
one atmospheric device, and the fixed-stage rules. Never label a slide "custom" / "preset" /
"AI-generated" — the design speaks for itself.
